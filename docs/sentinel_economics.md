# VRYPTIC Sentinel Network: Decentralized Storage Strategy

### 1. The Sentinel Role
Sentinels are decentralized nodes operating on the Arweave AO ecosystem. They act as the "Permanent Witness" to the hardware attestations generated by the VRYPTIC hardware layer.

### 2. Network Growth & Airdrop Structure
The Sentinel network is designed to scale alongside hardware adoption:
* **Uptime Guarantee:** To ensure network resilience, a monthly floor of 50 $PROOF tokens is allocated to active nodes maintaining verified uptime.
* **Airdrop Distribution:** Initial network density is achieved through a targeted airdrop structure, prioritizing early adopters of the VRYPTIC hardware-to-ledger standard.

### 3. Asynchronous Resilience
Sentinels support Asynchronous Hardware Attestation (AHA). This allows the network to maintain provenance integrity even in zero-connectivity environments, with automated batch-syncing once connectivity to the Arweave gateway is restored.
