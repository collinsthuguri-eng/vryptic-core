VRYPTIC Sentinel Network: Decentralized Storage Strategy
1. The Sentinel Role
Sentinels are decentralized nodes operating on the Arweave AO ecosystem. They act as the "Permanent Witness" to the hardware attestations generated by the VRYPTIC hardware layer.
2. Network Incentive Structure
The Sentinel network is designed to scale alongside hardware adoption:
Uptime Guarantee: Active Sentinel Nodes maintaining verified uptime earn a guaranteed monthly floor of $VRYP tokens. This floor is non-negotiable and is funded by a dedicated Sentinel Acquisition Fund, ensuring network operators are rewarded from day one — independent of TaaS revenue.
Burn-and-Mint Equilibrium (BME): A portion of all Truth-as-a-Service (TaaS) verification fees are burned, reducing $VRYP supply. The remainder recycles into the Sentinel Rewards pool, creating a direct link between enterprise verification demand and node operator income.
Airdrop Distribution: Initial network density is achieved through a targeted $VRYP airdrop structure, prioritizing early adopters of the VRYPTIC hardware-to-ledger standard.
3. Sentinel Tiers
Phase 1 — Bootstrap: The monthly uptime floor is open to early verified Sentinel Nodes on a first-come, first-verified basis. This unified reward structure simplifies onboarding and accelerates network growth.
Phase 2 — Sustainability: Upon reaching network maturity, new participants enter a Standby Queue governed by a Sentinel Reputation Score. High-performing nodes remain Active; underperforming nodes rotate out. Treasury-subsidized rewards gradually transition to Truth Royalties from institutional TaaS API fees.
4. Asynchronous Resilience
Sentinels support Asynchronous Hardware Attestation (AHA). This allows the network to maintain provenance integrity even in zero-connectivity environments, with automated batch-syncing once connectivity to the Arweave gateway is restored.
